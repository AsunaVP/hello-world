Chocolate Mug Brownie

This makes a delicious brownie for one in about a minute. I used a 1500 watt microwave. 60 seconds makes a molten center brownie. 75 seconds make a uniformly done brownie. Careful not to overcook.

Find the original recipe here

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz simple syrup (1:1)
  • 1 egg white
  • Angostura or Amargo bitters

Preparation

Combine all liquid ingredients, except the bitters, in a cocktail shaker. Shake vigorously for at least ten seconds. Add the ice and shake for a full minute. It's important to get it frothy! Strain the liquid into a cocktail glass. Top the foam, which is caused by the egg white, with a dash of the bitters.
